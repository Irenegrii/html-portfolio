The best series according to my opinion

My Top-3 of all the times

Game of Thrones

This epic fantasy series is based on George R.R. Martin's books and is one of the most popular TV series in television history.
It offers a complex plot, memorable characters, and thrilling battles in the fictional world of Westeros.

Breaking Bad

This series tells the story of Walter White, an average chemist who becomes a drug lord after being diagnosed with a deadly illness.
"Breaking Bad" is known for its intricate plot twists and deep character drama.

Stranger Things

This popular science fiction series is set in the 1980s and combines elements of drama, thriller, and the supernatural. It investigates the disappearance of a boy and mysterious events in their small town, connected to a parallel world and supernatural forces.

In addition to these, there are many other excellent series in various genres, and the choice depends on your preferences.
